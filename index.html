About me

When I was a child growing up in a small town, I had a passion for creating things. Whether it was palmetto forts in the backyard, a home movie about Furbies trying to take over the house, or a comic book about Garfield finally leaving Jon and joining a ragtag team of misfit cats who liked harassing dogs at dog parks, I felt I needed to find ways to entertain myself in my otherwise boring town. Today I still have that passion. While it doesn’t involve the Furby toy by Tiger Electronics or Garfield the cat, I like to create things when I can such as music, screenplays, cross-stitches, bread, movies, and much more.

Since graduating from UCF with a film degree in 2012, I’ve had a few odd jobs not related to film. Most of them were mind-numbing work with little to no creativity. I knew I wanted a job with a mix of hands-on technical stuff and creativity, so when I spoke with my friend who was in the web development field, I ran home and started teaching myself HTML and CSS. Two weeks later, I finally took a break to eat and shower and now I think I know what I want to do for a living.

My name is Nick, and I currently live in the Orlando area with my girlfriend Jen and our pug Simon. I recently finished a Full Stack coding bootcamp at UCF, and I'm currently looking for employement to become a Front End or Full Stack Web Developer. I am excited to continue to learn and grow in this industry.
